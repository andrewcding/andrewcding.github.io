about me

I am an aspiring geospatial data scientist based in Toronto, Canada, driven by the desire to leverage geographic information in the form of effective data visualization for positive global impact. My passions within in the intersection of geography, statistics, and urbanism.

I have showcased my geographic expertise on the global stage, participating in international geography competitions. Moreover, my work experience encompasses internships across research, public and private sectors, where I have honed my skills in GIS and data science.

In my free time, you might find me planning my next trip. I have been to 38 different countries and have previously lived in Lausanne, Switzerland and Singapore. I also enjoy hiking, playing board games, and browsing through maps.
